Travel to Poland

Poland, officially the Republic of Poland, is a country in Central Europe. It extends from the Baltic Sea in the north to the Sudetes and Carpathian Mountains in the south, bordered by Lithuania and Russia to the northeast, Belarus and Ukraine to the east, Slovakia and the Czech Republic to the south, and Germany to the west.

  2. Lodz , Łódź Voivodeship 768,755
  3. Krakow , Lesser Poland 755,050
  4. Wroclaw , Lower Silesia 634,893

Polish food

Polish cuisine is really tasty. Here are some of the most popular polish dishes.

  1. Complementary traditional Polish farmers food (bigos stew, pierogi dumplings, gołąbki cabbage rolls, skwarki cracklings)
  2. Various kinds of Polish kielbasa. From the top down: biała, kabanos, wiejska with mustard
  3. Oscypek, a Polish smoked cheese and traditional food of the Goral people in the Tatra Mountains
  4. Bagels originated in Poland and became widespread during the migration of Polish Jews.
